What is QKnow Edtech?

Quick Knowedge Edtech is a data driven educational software that aims to deliver Quality Education to all stakeholders of Education. We are using technology and media to deliver our educational content so that students can gain access to Books, Articles, Quizzes, Videos, activities and games on demand.

Student Success
We believe that through the use of learning data and analytical tools, we will be able to identify students that are at risk of failing at a faster rate which will help us work with their schools and teachers to ensure that this is rectified, and general student success is achieved.
Academic Potential
We are providing a platform that gives students the chance to realize their true academic potential and unearth their talents so that they can further lead development and advancement of this planet. Hence our motto; Learn Today, Lead Tomorrow.
Learning Communities
We want to connect students from all parts of the world through a learning community that goes beyond the countries they come from or the educational system they find themselves in. We believe in the power of networking at an early age, where healthy competition and collaboration can ensure that Earth becomes a Global village with diverse ideas and contributions.
Equal Learning Opportunities
Our life long mission is to ensure that every student gets access to Quality Education, irrespective of their financial background. In Africa, the urban-rural divide has created a long lasting disparity between the type of education and resources that rural areas receive. QKnow wants to work with partners that have education at heart to provide a level playing field so that these students in rural areas can contribute to bringing their families and communities out of poverty thereby leading a new generation that can develop these areas.
