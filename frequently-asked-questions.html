QKnow Edtech Frequently Asked Questions

What is QKnow?

QKnow is an education technology company that aims to improve the delivery of quality education for all stakeholders in the educational sector.

Our web app hosts thousands of round-the-clock entertaining, engaging, and in-depth educational video lessons delivered by expert tutors.

Our lessons are delivered in pre-recorded (asynchronous) video formats, giving learners an experience that is unprecedented in its richness and interactivity. Our product aims to deliver better learning outcomes and improve learners' academic performance across Africa and the globe.

How do I sign up?

You can sign up for the ‘QKnow’ web app at www.qknowedtech.com to register an account.

I am not writing any WASSCE, BECE, or any regional or national exams. Is QKnow still suitable for me?

Absolutely! While QKnow’s vast library of national and regional past question papers and solutions makes it an ideal product in helping to prepare a learner for critical exams, our service is much more than a test-preparation tool. Our thousands of curriculum-relevant books, quizzes, video tutorials etc are designed to provide learners with a better and richer learning experience by personalizing the way they learn and making the process fun and enriching.

Data Retention

We will not retain student personal information for any longer than is necessary for educational purposes and legal obligations, or to provide the Service for which we receive or collect the student's personal information

What are the benefits of using QKnow?

There are many benefits to using QKnow, key among these are:

Access to thousands of high-quality, curriculum-relevant books, notes, quizzes, video tutorials, which are further enhanced by the use of digital animation and illustration to help a learner grasp critical – and sometimes complex – concepts;

The ability of a learner to learn at his/her own pace using the pre-recorded library;

Well-detailed learning analytics that allows a learner, as well as their parents/guardians, to monitor and assess progress across subjects and/or specific topics;

Weekly reports sent via SMS & WhatsApp app to parents/guardians. Preparation for upcoming exams by sitting for mock exams. A fun and exciting new way to learn, which complements more traditional forms of teaching.
