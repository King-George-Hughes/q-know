What We Provide

All the amazing things we provide and more

Student Learning Accounts

Students can easily signup and navigate the wide range of content we have on QKnow Edtech in various forms; Books and articles, Quizzes, Videos, Worksheets and more.

BECE Prepkit

Candidates preparing for the BECE can prepare the right way! Students can take timed objective past questions with instant results. The BECE Prepkit also has Video Tutorials and solutions to Section B Questions that students generally struggle with. Candidates can take online Mocks each month to help them prepare well.

Teacher Maven Account

Teachers are integral to the delivery of Quality Education, which is why QKnow Edtech has given teachers the right tools and resources to create educational content. Teachers can share their knowledge in different ways, save time with the self-marking feature, teach remotely with video and finally monitor their students’ academic performances in real time.

School Account

Heads of Schools and Administrators can now monitor the delivery of Quality Education in their Schools. They can assign students and teachers to classes, closely follow performances of both teachers and students and step in to offer solutions in real time when there are problems. With QKnow Edtech, schools can access archived student results to quickly asses each students’ academic trajectory over time.

Parents

Stay on top of your child’s progress, Read and review school or teacher announcements. View lessons and quizzes your child has attempted and check in their academic progress or competitions and projects they might have entered.

Q-Know Activity Boxes

We curate activity boxes that your students can use offline. Buy themed boxes that aim to test the creative potential of kids whilst testing their intelligence.
